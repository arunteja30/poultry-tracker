🛏️ Bedding Tips

🌾 Best Materials

Rice husk, wood shavings, chopped straw, sand, paper shreds.

🧪 Powders/Additives

Lime powder (thin layer), diatomaceous earth (food-grade), bioenzymes/probiotics.

📏 Layering

Spread bedding 2–4 inches thick. For chicks, use 1–2 inches; for adults, 3–4 inches.

🥄 Powder Application

Sprinkle lime/diatomaceous earth before bedding and mix a little into bedding, especially in damp areas.

🔄 Turning

Stir bedding daily, remove wet/caked spots, add fresh bedding and powder as needed.

💧 Moisture Control

Keep bedding dry, use fans/ventilation to reduce humidity.

📐 Depth Adjustment

Increase thickness in cold weather, reduce if bedding is too compact.

🧽 Cleaning

Remove all bedding and disinfect between flocks, reapply powders before new bedding.

⚠️ Safety

Avoid overusing powders, use only untreated wood, monitor ammonia smell.
