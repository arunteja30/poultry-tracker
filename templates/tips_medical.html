🏥 Medical Tips: Common Diseases & Remedies

🦠 Disease Management Guide
Disease Symptoms Natural Remedies Vet-Recommended Medicines
Coccidiosis 🩸 Bloody droppings, weakness, ruffled feathers 🌿 Mix turmeric (½ tsp/liter water), keep litter dry, add garlic to water 💊 Amprolium, Sulfa drugs (as per vet dosage)
Newcastle Disease (Ranikhet) 🫁 Respiratory distress, greenish diarrhea, paralysis 🌿 Neem leaf water, tulsi water for immunity 💉 Vaccination (Lasota, F strain), supportive antibiotics
Infectious Bursal Disease (IBD/Gumboro) 🔴 Swollen vent, watery diarrhea, depression 🌿 Amla juice in water, aloe vera juice for immunity 💉 Vaccination, supportive care (electrolytes, vitamins)
Fowl Pox 🔍 Wart-like lesions on comb/wattles, drop in egg production 🌿 Apply turmeric paste on lesions, keep birds isolated 💉 Vaccination, iodine/glycerin topical application
Respiratory Infections (CRD, Coryza) 👁️ Swollen eyes, nasal discharge, sneezing 🌿 Ginger juice in water, tulsi leaves, improve ventilation 💊 Tylosin, oxytetracycline, as per vet advice
Worms (Roundworm, Tapeworm) ⚖️ Weight loss, pale comb, diarrhea 🌿 Papaya seeds (crushed, small amount in feed), neem leaf water 💊 Piperazine, albendazole, fenbendazole (as per vet dosage)
External Parasites (Mites, Lice) 🐛 Itching, feather loss, restlessness 🌿 Diatomaceous earth dusting, neem oil spray 💊 Permethrin spray, ivermectin (as per vet advice)
⚠️ Important: Always consult a veterinarian for correct diagnosis and dosage. Use natural remedies as supplements, not replacements for professional treatment.
🌿 Prevention First

Good hygiene and proper nutrition prevent most diseases.

🏥 Early Detection

Daily observation helps catch diseases early for better outcomes.

💊 Professional Help

Always consult a veterinarian for serious conditions.
